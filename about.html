ABOUT CAROL

Carol hails from the City of Windsor, Ontario ,Canada. She attended the University of Windsor. She did many courses at the University in Drama, Arts and Education. Carol obtained an Honours in Drama and a Bachelors of Education. She is a well known and respected member of her community. Carol was always interested in Arts, Drawing, Sketching and Painting. Before the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, her interest in being an artist was at the forefront for a new career and she has continued to do so on a daily basis since. She is looking forward to having her paintings in various galleries, and continues to develop her skills as an Abstract Painter. “When you see my art and my collection , I am trusting that you will want to have one of my paintings hanging on your wall to inspire you. I am learning to align myself , my mind, my heart, my soul and my strength with my spirit. My art has helped me to do this and it gets easier everyday.”- Carol My body of work continues to grow on a daily basis and with every brush stroke, I am inspired to create original abstract painting on canvas. We are the light of the world, you and I .Some paintings,when you see them, will move you to share with your friends and families and on social media. I promise you that my paintings will get you to move and search your heart and minds to do the things you love and you will share your talent with others in the near future.
